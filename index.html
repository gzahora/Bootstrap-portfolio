About Me


My name is Greg Zahora and after 4 years as a financial analyst, working in interest rate/liquidity risk and equity research, I am working on becoming a software developer. I noticed in my previous career that I was most engaged when I was automating excel spreadsheets and creating complicated nested IF statements, leading me to the realization that I was in the wrong industry. This led me to quit finance all together and work to become a software developer.

In my free time I like to be outdoors hiking and camping; I try to spend one or two weeks a year on a major hiking/camping trip. I've been to eight North American national parks. My favorites parks so far are Zion National Park and Banff National Park.

I try to spend my more-limited daily free time keeping up with current events and geopolitical affairs. It's a goal of mine to be informed enough to be able to have a conversation about any subject. I'm getting close... but am lacking most in the pop-culture department.
